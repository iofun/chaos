SHA/OS

Thu 29 June 2017

SHA/OS is a nonsense.ws sponsored project.

Our primary user base is within the distributed high-performance computing community. However, our users come from a wide variety of industries and use cases.

Our Mission: Driven by nonsense.ws we focus on multi-dimensional computer science research, shaos.ws provide a linux workspace environment full of distributed asynchronous tools.

Our Goal: Provide a stable, scalable, extensible, distributed environment for kids of all ages.

The Linux distribution is based on Debian plus the nonsense.ws environment we provide chat and voice assistance in a tiling window user interface, there is heavy focus on integration with the BEAM Erlang/OTP VM and the official native implementation of ZeroMQ that enable zillions of Lua processes to communicate with torch, pytorch, openresty, love2d, tornado, turbo and all our Erlang, Python and Lua friends, we bring you a small box full of tools for the cloud forest jungle.

We make a stable workspace for distributed high-performance computing that anyone can use today, at nonsense.ws we put these things together into one open-source unified environment.
